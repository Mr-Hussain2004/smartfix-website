⚠️ The Problem
Traditional Maintenance
Companies Are Broken
Administrative chaos plagues the industry — lost customers, wasted time, and zero accountability. We identified four critical failure points.
📩

Request Chaos

Customer requests arrive via random, unorganized WhatsApp messages that are nearly impossible to track, document, or prioritize effectively.

🎲

Random Dispatching

Technicians are sent to locations without any consideration of distance or current workload, causing severe and avoidable response delays.

👁

Lack of Oversight

Technicians can manipulate their working hours — fake timestamps, unjustified pauses — while management has no reliable way to track real performance.

📝

Unprofessional Reports

Informal, poorly written maintenance notes damage the company's professional image and undermine customer trust at the final billing stage.

✨ Core Features
Not Just an App —
An Automated Operations Manager

Six intelligent modules working in concert to eliminate inefficiency, prevent fraud, and deliver a flawless customer experience.

01
🧠

AI Magic Extract

Paste any raw WhatsApp message from a customer and our Gemini-powered AI instantly extracts the name, issue, and address — converting it into an official tracked task in under one second.

Gemini AI · NLP
02
📡

Smart Dispatch & Routing

An intelligent algorithm calculates precise geographical distances and analyzes each technician's real-time workload — automatically assigning every task to the closest and most available technician.

Geolocation · Smart Routing
03
👁️

Live Operations Radar

An interactive Kanban board paired with an emergency radar detects delays, interruptions, and critical issues as they emerge — giving management complete situational awareness in real-time.

Real-Time · Kanban
04
🛡️

Smart SLA & Fraud Detection

An advanced engine monitors technicians' time, prevents Fake GPS apps from spoofing location, and automatically issues Red Flags and financial deductions for time manipulation or unjustified pauses without photographic evidence.

Anti-Fraud · SLA Engine
05
🪄

AI Formal Reports

Technicians' informal written or voice notes are automatically transformed into polished, highly professional engineering reports — ready to be included in the customer's final invoice.

Voice-to-Text · AI Writing
06
🧾

Digital Invoicing & WhatsApp

Generate professional PDF invoices with a verified digital customer signature and instantly dispatch them — along with a satisfaction rating link — directly to the customer via WhatsApp in a single tap.

PDF · WhatsApp · E-Signature
🔄 Workflow
From Request to Invoice
in Minutes

A seamless, fully automated pipeline that handles every stage of the maintenance lifecycle without manual intervention.

1

Customer Sends WhatsApp Message

A customer describes their issue in natural language via WhatsApp — no forms, no portals, no friction.

2

AI Extracts & Creates Task

Gemini AI parses the message, extracts the name, issue, and location, and instantly creates a tracked task in the system.

3

Smart Dispatch Assigns Technician

The routing algorithm selects the optimal technician based on proximity and current workload, then auto-assigns the job.

4

Live Monitoring & Fraud Detection

The operations radar tracks progress in real-time while the SLA engine monitors time integrity and flags any anomalies.

5

AI Generates Professional Report

The technician's field notes are converted into a formal engineering report, automatically appended to the job record.

6

Invoice Sent & Rating Collected

A signed PDF invoice is generated and sent via WhatsApp with a rating link — closing the loop professionally and instantly.
